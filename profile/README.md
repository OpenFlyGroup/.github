## 🎉 Our Projects

### 📅 WeTask

**WeTask** is your ultimate companion for tracking tasks. Whether you're flying solo, or managing a team, this app has you covered! 🌍
- **Busyness Journal** 📋: Schedule tasks, categorize them (work, personal, family), and sync with team.
- **Workload Index** ⚖️: Analyze your task distribution to avoid burnout.
- **Team Sync** 👨‍👩‍👧: Collaborate with your partner or team to align schedules and boost connection.
- **Gamification** 🏆: Earn achievements, complete challenges, and level up to stay motivated!
